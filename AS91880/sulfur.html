Farming sulfur


Sulfur looks like a stone node with yellow lines running through it. These give up to 300 sulfur per node. Sulfur is used in the production of explosive such as gunpowder. Gunpowder is used in the making Ammunition, explosive ammunition, bean cans, satchels, and c4.

Tool infomation

Farming in rust is a base aspect of it gathering materials to make a base or tools for your farming

There are three tairs of tool in rust

  1. Mechanical , gathers the fastest
  2. Salvaged , double the time of the mechanical
  3. Metal , double the time of the salvaged
  4. Stone , same time as metal
