Farming metal

The metal node looks like stone node bust slightly darker and shiny. These give you metal ore and high-quality metal ore which you smelt to get metal frags and high-quality metal.
These are used for all sorts of thing like base upgrades building placeables, ammunition, guns, and meds.

Tool infomation

Farming in rust is a base aspect of it gathering materials to make a base or tools for your farming

There are three tairs of tool in rust

  1. Mechanical , gathers the fastest
  2. Salvaged , double the time of the mechanical
  3. Metal , double the time of the salvaged
  4. Stone , same time as metal
